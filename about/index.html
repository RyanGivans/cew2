About CEW

Design. Build. Maintain. Outdoor spaces built to last.

Established in 2020, CEW Landscape is a Northwest Arkansas design-build-maintain landscape company focused on project-specific, customer-driven landscape solutions and high-quality installations.

Company Approach

Thoughtful design, proper construction, and dependable long-term care.

Every project begins with understanding the client’s vision and translating it into a functional, beautiful, and sustainable landscape.

CEW takes pride in quality materials installed with care, precision, and craftsmanship. Site conditions, sunlight, drainage, irrigation, and long-term maintenance needs are evaluated so the finished project performs well in its environment.

Founder

Chad E. Walters, BLA

Founder & Owner, CEW Landscape

Chad E. Walters is the founder and owner of CEW Landscape. Originally from Hot Springs, Arkansas, Chad moved to Northwest Arkansas in 1995 to pursue a degree in Landscape Architecture at the University of Arkansas. After earning a Bachelor of Landscape Architecture in 2000, he began a career spanning more than two decades in the landscape and engineering industries.

His background includes landscape design, project management, construction, operations, business leadership, and years of hands-on experience overseeing crews, client relationships, and large-scale landscape projects.

Company Story

Founded in 2020 with a complete landscape solution in mind.

Mission

To create and maintain exceptional outdoor spaces that enhance the lives of clients while delivering lasting value through design expertise, quality craftsmanship, and dependable service.

Vision

To be Northwest Arkansas’s premier landscape design, build, and maintenance company, known for excellence, innovation, and integrity in every project.

As a design-build-maintain company, CEW combines professional design expertise, quality construction, and dependable maintenance services under one roof. The company continues to grow while staying true to exceptional service, thoughtful design, quality workmanship, and long-term relationships.
