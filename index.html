Our Process

Clear, organized, and professional from start to finish.

CEW Landscape guides each project from the first conversation through final walkthrough, keeping the process simple and transparent.

Initial Contact

We learn about your property, goals, timeline, and outdoor needs.

On-Site Meeting

We walk the property, discuss ideas, identify challenges, and understand the vision.

Site Assessment

We evaluate sunlight, drainage, grading, irrigation, plant health, access, and maintenance needs.

Design Work

When needed, we create a design or plan to help visualize the project before installation.

Estimate

We prepare a detailed estimate based on scope, materials, labor, and goals.

Review

We meet to review the design and estimate, answer questions, and confirm the direction.

Revisions

If adjustments are needed, we revise the design or estimate before final approval.

Final Approval

Once approved, we finalize details and prepare the agreement.

Contract & Deposit

The client signs the contract and submits the deposit to secure the project.

Scheduling

We coordinate installation timing based on materials, weather, and project scope.

Project Completion

Our team completes the work with attention to detail, quality materials, and professional workmanship.

Final Walkthrough

We conduct a detailed walkthrough, confirm approval, and review plant care, watering schedules, and aftercare.

Free estimates are available. When design work is needed, design costs may be deducted from the final installation project.
About

About CEW Landscape

Established in 2020, CEW Landscape is a Northwest Arkansas-based design-build-maintain landscape company focused on delivering project-specific, customer-driven landscape solutions and high-quality installations.

We specialize in creating outdoor spaces that are thoughtfully designed, properly constructed, and built to last. Every project begins with understanding the client’s vision and translating it into a functional, beautiful, and sustainable landscape.

We take pride in the use of quality materials installed with care, precision, and craftsmanship. Our team is intentional in every detail, from layout and grading to plant selection and placement.

We evaluate site conditions, sunlight, drainage, long-term maintenance needs, soil conditions when appropriate, and irrigation coverage to support healthy landscapes and efficient watering.

Throughout every phase, communication is a top priority. Upon completion, we conduct a detailed walkthrough and review plant care, watering schedules, and aftercare recommendations.

About the Founder

Chad E. Walters, BLA
Founder & Owner, CEW Landscape

Chad E. Walters is the founder and owner of CEW Landscape, a full-service design, build, and maintenance landscape company serving Northwest Arkansas.

Originally from Hot Springs, Arkansas, Chad moved to Northwest Arkansas in 1995 to pursue a degree in Landscape Architecture at the University of Arkansas. After earning a Bachelor of Landscape Architecture in 2000, he began a career spanning more than two decades in the landscape and engineering industries.

Throughout his career, Chad has gained experience in landscape design, project management, construction, operations, and business leadership. His professional journey also took him to Denver, where he spent three years coaching high school baseball while continuing to work as a landscape designer.

After returning to Arkansas, Chad managed and operated a large landscape company for several years, gaining extensive experience overseeing crews, client relationships, business operations, and large-scale landscape projects. In 2020, he launched CEW Landscape with a commitment to quality, professionalism, and customer service.

Today, Chad continues to lead the company with a hands-on approach, focusing on lasting relationships, talented teams, and landscapes that enhance the beauty, functionality, and value of every property.

Company Story, Mission & Vision

Founded in 2020, CEW Landscape was built on a simple idea: provide clients with a complete landscape solution from concept to completion and beyond.

As a design-build-maintain company, CEW Landscape combines professional design expertise, quality construction, and dependable maintenance services under one roof. Serving residential and commercial clients across Northwest Arkansas, CEW Landscape specializes in landscape design, installation, renovation, hardscapes, irrigation, and maintenance programs tailored to each property’s unique needs.

What began as a new venture during one of the most challenging periods in recent history has grown into a thriving business. Through craftsmanship, reliability, and customer satisfaction, CEW Landscape has established itself as a trusted partner for homeowners, businesses, property managers, and developers throughout the region.
